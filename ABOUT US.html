Interview Practice.

Worklearn’s interview practice questions guides users to questions commonly found in applying for a job, these exercises not only tests the user’s ability to answer but gives them a sense of idea what to expect in applying

IQ Test.

Worklearn has a IQ test feature to determine the user’s IQ, this test can help the company on hiring and can give them a sense of idea how the user does their work

Resume Builder.

WorkLearn's Resume Builder is a powerful tool designed to help students create professional, eye-catching resumes with ease. Tailored specifically for those entering the workforce, it guides users through a simple yet comprehensive process to highlight their skills, experiences, and academic achievements. With customizable templates and easy-to-follow prompts, students can build a resume that showcases their strengths and stands out to potential employers. Whether it's for internships, part-time jobs, or full-time opportunities, WorkLearn ensures that students have the resources they need to present themselves confidently and professionally
